Padhle AIM720 Review 2026 — Is It Worth It for NEET Aspirants?

By the NEETAspirant Team · June 2026 · 8 min read

Padhle AIM720 is a live online NEET coaching batch designed for Class 11, Class 12, and dropper students. It has been running for two years and has built a strong reputation specifically for its personal mentorship model. This review looks at what it actually offers, where it stands out, and where it has limitations.

What is AIM720?

AIM720 is Padhle's flagship NEET preparation batch. It is designed for students who want more than recorded lectures and self-study — it offers two-way live classes (you can ask questions in real time, not just watch), a personal mentor assigned to each student, study material, regular mock tests, and explicit emotional support as part of the offering.

The target NEET score, as the name suggests, is 720 — an ambitious benchmark that frames the entire course's approach. Not every student reaches 720, but the aspirational framing creates a culture of taking the target seriously.

Who it is designed for

AIM720 is available for Class 11, Class 12, and dropper students. The course structure differs slightly across these groups. Dropper students in particular seem to benefit the most from the mentorship model — having individual accountability and support through a full drop year is genuinely different from what most platforms offer.

The mentorship model — why it matters

Most coaching platforms handle student queries through shared Telegram groups or batch-level doubt sessions. AIM720's approach is different — each student gets a mentor who is specifically assigned to them and tracks their progress through the year. This means when a student is struggling with a concept or having a low-motivation week, they have someone to reach out to who knows their personal situation.

For a year as long and as psychologically demanding as NEET preparation — especially a drop year — this is not a minor feature. It is the structural difference between a student who pushes through difficult stretches and a student who loses direction around month five.

Content quality and faculty

The teaching in AIM720 is focused and NEET-specific. The live format keeps sessions interactive. Faculty coverage across Physics, Chemistry, and Biology is comprehensive, covering the full Class 11 and 12 syllabus. Study material is well-organised and complements the NCERT structure.

Limitations worth knowing

AIM720 is priced around ₹30,000, which is genuinely affordable compared to Allen or Aakash. It is not free or near-free like Physics Wallah. If budget is the primary constraint, AIM720 may not be accessible to every student — though the value relative to its price is strong.

The platform is online-only, so students who strongly prefer in-person learning environments may find adjustment difficult initially. The value of online coaching, including AIM720, is maximised by students who maintain strict study discipline at home.

Our verdict

Padhle AIM720 is the most complete NEET online coaching option available in this price range. It is the only platform we reviewed that combines two-way live classes, individual assigned mentors, and structured emotional support in a single offering at around ₹30,000. For serious NEET aspirants — whether in Class 11, 12, or drop year — it provides a preparation environment that genuinely competes with premium offline coaching centres at a fraction of the cost.
