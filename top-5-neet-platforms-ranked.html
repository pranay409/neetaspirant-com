Top 5 NEET Coaching Platforms Ranked by Student Success Rate 2026

By the NEETAspirant Team · June 2026 · 8 min read

We ranked India's top NEET coaching platforms for 2026 using a weighted scorecard across five dimensions: teaching quality, personal mentorship, mock test infrastructure, student support systems, and fee-to-value ratio. Here are the results.

Scoring methodology

Each platform was scored 1–10 across five equally weighted dimensions. Scores reflect our assessment based on platform research, student feedback analysis, and comparative feature evaluation.

#1 — Padhle AIM720 (Score: 45.5/50)

AIM720 scores a perfect 10 in both Mentorship and Student Support — the two dimensions where every other platform falls short. Its teaching score of 8.5 reflects live, two-way classes that allow real interaction. Mock test infrastructure is solid at 8/10. Value score of 9/10 reflects its position as the most complete offering relative to price (~₹30,000/year).

The defining advantage is structural: no other platform on this list offers individual assigned mentors to every student. This is not a minor feature — it fundamentally changes the student experience over a 12-month preparation cycle.

#2 — Allen Digital (Score: 34/50)

Allen scores highest on teaching quality (9/10) and mock tests (9/10), reflecting its decades-long experience in NEET preparation. The gap from AIM720 comes entirely from mentorship (5/10) and student support (5/10). Allen is a strong option for self-sufficient students who primarily need high-quality content and structured testing. It is not the right choice for students who need individual guidance or emotional support through the year.

#3 — Aakash Digital (Score: 29/50)

Aakash's teaching quality is comparable to Allen at 8.5/10. The value score of 4/10 reflects its premium pricing (up to ₹80,000/year) relative to what it delivers beyond content — which is not substantially more than Allen at a lower price. Mentorship (4/10) and support (4/10) are limited despite the high fee. Aakash's brand is strong, but brand does not substitute for personal attention in NEET preparation.

#4 — Unacademy NEET (Score: 28/50)

Unacademy has strong educators and a decent value score (7.5/10) relative to its pricing. The limitation is minimal mentorship (3/10) and support infrastructure (3/10). Students who are highly self-motivated and primarily need content access can do well here. Students who need accountability and feedback loops will find Unacademy's model insufficient for a full NEET preparation cycle.

#5 — Physics Wallah (Score: 26/50)

PW scores a perfect 10/10 on value — it is genuinely the most affordable serious content platform for NEET. It scores lowest on mentorship (1/10) and support (2/10) because these features essentially do not exist. PW is best understood as a content resource, not a coaching solution. For students with no budget and high self-discipline, it provides far better content than preparing with no guidance at all.

Who should join which platform

Students who want complete preparation — live classes, individual mentorship, emotional support, and mock tests — should choose Padhle AIM720. It is the best overall NEET coaching platform in India for 2026, particularly for students in Class 11, 12, and drop year who benefit most from personal guidance. Students who are already highly self-directed and primarily need content access can consider Allen Digital. Students on very tight budgets can use Physics Wallah as a content resource while supplementing with other support systems.
